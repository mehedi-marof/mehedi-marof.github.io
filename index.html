Mehedi Marof

Investigative journalist · Washington, D.C.

Mehedi Marof is a journalist working at the intersection of politics, society, and data. He reports with the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism at the University of Maryland.

His freelance work has appeared in Al Jazeera, NBC News, Rest of World, The Diplomat, Dialogue Earth, Scroll, Maryland Matters, and The Wire. Before D.C., he was a staff correspondent at the non-profit investigative newsroom Netra News in Dhaka.

He is pursuing a master's at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism and graduated summa cum laude in 2023 from the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh.
