More ↓

Mehedi Marof is an investigative journalist from Dhaka, Bangladesh currently based in Washington, D.C., and reporting with the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism.

His freelance reporting has appeared in publications including Al Jazeera, NBC News, Rest of World, The Diplomat, Dialogue Earth, Scroll, Maryland Matters, and The Wire. He previously was a reporter with the nonprofit investigative newsroom Netra News in Dhaka.

Marof is now pursuing a master of journalism (M.J.) at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at University of Maryland. He graduated summa cum laude in 2023 in journalism from the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh, and received the Vice Chancellor's Award and the Dean's Award. He also was a research assistant at the Center for Sustainable Development.
